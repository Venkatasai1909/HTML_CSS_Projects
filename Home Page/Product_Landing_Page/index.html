Specs



            Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G
            Our Note Series has been received with a lot of love & appreciation in the past, so we are extremely proud to be presenting 
            the next big thing, Redmi Note 12 Series. Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G features the flagship 50MP Sony IMX766 sensor with OIS, 
            120Hz Pro AMOLED Display, a brand new processor Mediatek Dimensity 1080 with 10 5G Band support and 67W Turbo Charge.
            
            Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G Features: 
            The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with the flagship 50MP Sony IMX766 Sensor 
            Setup: 50MP Primary Camera + 8MP Ultrawide Camera + 2MP Macro Camera. 
            It also features OIS for the first time ever on a Redmi Note for stable-blur free pictures for exceptional low-light photography. 
            These features provide clear & detailed images even in the most challenging lighting situations. 
            Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with a 120Hz Pro AMOLED Display with Dolby Atmos & Vision support. 
            This combination along with HDR10+, a 1 billion color display, dual stereo speakers, and hi-res audio (wired & wireless) certification 
            transforms your device into a wholesome multimedia package.
            The brand-new processor, Mediatek Dimensity 1080 comes packed with power. And features numerous upgrades over the previous generation.
            Experience fast charging with 67W Turbo Charge and get 51% charge in 15 minutes which is enough to keep you going for the day 
            along with the massive 5000mAh battery.

Frequently Asked Questions



            1. Can I expand the storage on the Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G? 
            Ans. No, there is no microSD slot on the Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G.
            2. Should I put a screen guard on the screen?
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 for protection on the front. However for additional protection you can put a screen guard.
            3. Does the device support 5G?
            Ans. Yes, the Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with 5G support for all the 10 relevant bands for out-of-the-box support for 5G in India. 
            4.What kind of display does the Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G have?
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G features a 6.67 120Hz Super AMOLED Display capable of producing 1Billion+ colors, alongwith a touch sampling rate of 240Hz making each scroll and swipe super smooth & seamless. 
            5. Can I use my wired headphones with Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G?
            Ans. Yes, the device comes with a dedicated 3.5 mm audio jack that can be used with your wired & headphones alongwith support for bluetooth for your wireless ones. 
            6. What version of Android does it run?
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G runs MIUI 13 based on Android 12.
            7. What is the processor on the device?
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with the latest processor Mediatek Dimensity 1080 along with enhanced software capabilities to help deliver the best output possible. 
            8. What is the camera set-up on the device?
            Ans. Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G packs an Sony IMX766 processor with OIS alongwith a 8MP ultra wide camera and a 2MP macro camera.
            9. Does the Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G come with Turbo Charge? 
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G comes with 67W Turbo Charging support alongwith a 67W Turbo charger in box. It also features a massive battery of 5000mAh that further enhances the charging experience. 
            10. What are the variants availalable for the device? 
            Ans. The Redmi Note 12 Pro 5G is available in 2 RAM variants: 6GB and 8GB, and 2 ROM variants: 128GB and 256GB.
